Digital Strategy

“Failing To Plan Is Planning To Fail”
- Benjamin Franklin (supposedly)

We want to hear from you what you want to achieve in your business and we will determine how a website or mobile app will help you reach your objectives. We take the time to find the smartest and most cost effective route to the right tech solution for you.


UX/UI

- Frank Lloyd Wright

Next, we work out the right User Experience given the objectives of you website or mobile app, and design a beautiful User Interface to interacting with, so your customers keep engaging with your brand, your products and your services.


Development

“Developer: an organism that turns coffee into code.”
- Unknown

Time to code! Our developers build what we designed, using robust and forward-thinking technologies to ensure both stability and growth-readiness of your platform. We take special care to create a seamless online experience using any and all devices. Using the Agile development methodology, we release new features very often, so you can see the progress and give us your feedback as we build.


Testing

“No one in the brief history of computing has ever written a piece of perfect software. It's unlikely that you'll be the first.”
- Andy Hunt

We are following Test Driven Development, so we constantly test our code for bugs along the way. That being said, once we reach the final product, we will also allow for time for you to check it thoroughly and to tell us about potential bugs you find or small tweaks you may want. We won’t leave you with a product you’re not happy with.


ABOUT US

Jenga Tech is a digital agency founded in 2018. Our diversity of backgrounds is our strength; our passion for technology is our drive. Our team members have prior experience from building start-ups to working with the biggest global brands and multinational companies. We have studied from Fashion Design to Statistics and Computer Science. This enables us to not look at technology in silo, but as a solution for your business as a whole.

Integrity. Honesty. Transparency. We will earn your trust as your technology partner thanks to our open communication. We will tell you what we think is right for your business even if it means less work for us, how we are progressing on your platform, what is going well and what is not. You will not always like what we have to say, and vice versa. But that’s what partners do. They help and challenge one another to grow together.
